Blind Professionals Network, UNITING TO BREAK BARRIERS AND ACHIEVE PROFESSIONAL SUCCESS!

Mission

The mission of Blind Professionals Network (BPN) is to unite Blind and Visually Impaired individuals to empower one another by sharing professional knowledge and experiences, along with providing trainings and resources, to help maintain success in any professional industry. Together we can overcome workplace barriers, reach career goals, and achieve entrepreneurial success!

BPN is designed to benefit blind and visually impaired individuals that are:

  • Employed
  • Seeking Employment
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Exploring Career Options
  • Willing to Share Professional Knowledge and Wisdom

What BPN Offers

BPN offers a variety of services to assist you in achieving success; including, but not limited to:

  • Peer Support Group
  • Professional Empowerment Wednesday
  • Employment Services
  • Mentoring
  • Career Coaching
  • Resources
  • Directory of BVI Professionals
  • Networking Mixers
