Blind Professionals Network: Your Gateway to Essential Resources and Connections

At Blind Professionals Network (BPN), we are dedicated to fostering a thriving community where blind and visually impaired individuals can access the critical resources and connections needed to excel in their careers.1 We understand that success in the professional world extends beyond individual skills; it also relies on staying informed and building a robust network.

Member Resources through Our Email List:

As a valued BPN member, you gain access to a wealth of beneficial resources, services, and opportunities directly delivered to your inbox through our dedicated member email list.2 We meticulously curate and share information from a variety of reputable organizations, agencies, and companies that are specifically beneficial to the BVI community. This includes:

  • Employment Opportunities: Timely announcements of job openings, internships, and career fairs, often with employers actively seeking blind and visually impaired talent.
  • Educational Workshops & Trainings: Information on professional development programs, webinars, and training sessions from other organizations, covering topics such as new software, industry trends, and leadership skills.
  • Assistive Technology Updates: Alerts on the latest advancements in assistive technology, special offers on devices, and new training programs to enhance your productivity and accessibility.
  • Disability Resources & Advocacy: Information on rights, legal support, and advocacy initiatives that impact the BVI community in the workplace and beyond.
  • Community Services: Details on programs and services from other non-profits and agencies that offer support for independent living, transportation, and other aspects relevant to professionals who are blind or visually impaired.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Resources and opportunities for BVI individuals interested in starting or growing their own businesses.

Our goal is to ensure you are consistently informed and empowered with the tools and opportunities that can significantly impact your professional journey.

BPN Networking Mixers: Connecting Blind Professionals

Beyond sharing information, BPN actively facilitates meaningful connections through our highly anticipated Networking Mixers. These engaging events, often held virtually via Zoom conference calls, bring together blind and visually impaired professionals from diverse industries and experience levels. Our networking mixers are more than just social gatherings; they are strategic platforms for professional growth. They serve to:

  • Overcome Workplace Barriers: Provide a safe and relatable space for members to openly discuss challenges they may be experiencing in the workplace or during their job search. Members share invaluable tips, strategies, and solutions to overcome these barriers.
  • Share Knowledge and Experiences: Facilitate the exchange of professional knowledge, best practices, and personal experiences, allowing members to learn from each other's successes and navigate common hurdles.
  • Stay Current with Industry Trends: Discuss current events affecting the blind and visually impaired professional community, explore emerging assistive technologies, and identify trends that can enhance workplace experiences.
  • Discover Hidden Opportunities: Many job opportunities are filled through referrals and internal networks. Our mixers provide a direct avenue to learn about unadvertised positions and gain introductions to relevant contacts.
  • Build a Supportive Community: Foster a strong sense of community and peer support, reducing feelings of isolation and building confidence among participants.
  • Enhance Communication and Interpersonal Skills: Provide a low-pressure environment to practice networking etiquette, refine communication skills, and learn how to make a lasting impression.
  • Find Mentors and Allies: Connect aspiring professionals with seasoned veterans who can offer guidance, advice, and sponsorship. These relationships are crucial for navigating career changes, skill acquisition, and overall career progression.
  • Boost Professional Visibility: Increase your personal and professional visibility within the BVI community and beyond, establishing your reputation and expertise.

By participating in BPN's Networking Mixers, you are not just expanding your contact list; you are actively investing in your professional development, gaining insights, and building genuine relationships that can lead to long-term career success. Join us to connect, learn, and empower one another!
