Mission

Blind Professionals Network (BPN) is dedicated to equipping blind and visually impaired individuals with the comprehensive skills and knowledge needed to thrive in any professional industry. We achieve this by offering specialized assistive technology training, practical development workshops, tailored career coaching and mentorship, valuable internship opportunities, and a strong network for peer support and shared resources. BPN fosters a collaborative environment where members are empowered to overcome workplace barriers, achieve their career goals, and pursue entrepreneurial success.

What BPN Offers

BPN offers a variety of services to assist you in achieving success; including, but not limited to:

  • BPN Virtual Support Groups
  • Professional Empowerment Wednesday Presentations
  • Development Workshops
  • Assistive Technology Training
  • Employment Services
  • Career Mentoring Program
  • Internship Opportunities
  • BVI Resources and Networking Mixers

At Blind Professionals Network (BPN), we believe in empowering blind and visually impaired individuals without financial barriers. That's why all BPN services and programs are completely free for our members. Our commitment is to ensure you have every opportunity to gain the skills needed for success.

Becoming a member is simple: just complete our member form, and you will immediately begin receiving all member benefits. Membership and our comprehensive services will always remain free, and are exclusively tailored for blind and visually impaired individuals.

We are your one-stop shop to build confidence, acquire essential skills and knowledge, and so much more. We believe in your potential and are here to empower you every step of the way.
