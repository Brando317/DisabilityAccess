BPN Partners and Resources

Blind Professionals Network is proud to partner with organizations, agencies and business that support and enhance the blind and visually impaired community. We recognize the importance of sharing resources to unite and empower one another. Be sure to take advantage of the resources below and feel free to share with others!

Blind Accessibility Tips N/A N/A blindaccessibilitytips@gmail.com blindaccessibilitytips.com Blind Accessibility Tips is a website set up with tutorials to show blind and visually impaired people how to use their Windows computers and smartphones. The website is focused on the basics, getting persons with vision loss immediate assistance with learning how to use their devices as soon as they obtain them. The idea is that the blind person will build on the skill over time, after having first receive the basics through the website, and become excellent at using the device independently. The website, blindaccessibilitytips.com, is still being updated but still already has plenty of tutorials which consist of both text and videos.
