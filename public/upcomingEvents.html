Attending Blind Professionals Network workshops, presentations, and support groups is free to BPN members. Email invites will automatically be sent to members with specific event details. Wednesday May 18, 2022

Professional Empowerment Wednesday

Time: 7:00 PM CT

Place: BPN Zoom Platform

Join BPN for May’s PEW presentation “Effective Ways to Find and Get That Job!” facilitated by Sara Freeman Smith.

Sara Freeman Smith is an Employment Specialist with 30+ years of Human Resources & Talent Acquisition leadership experience working with major corporations and small businesses. She is visually impaired and will discuss job search & recruiting insights to help you land your ideal role. Sara will share perspectives from both sides of the desk -– as an employer hiring and as a job seeker. Come prepared to learn some helpful tips and ask your questions.
