Internship Opportunities at Blind Professionals Network

Blind Professionals Network (BPN) is committed to fostering the next generation of blind and visually impaired leaders and professionals. Our internship program offers a unique opportunity for individuals to gain invaluable real-world experience, develop critical professional skills, and contribute to an organization dedicated to empowering the blind and visually impaired community. We provide a supportive and accessible environment where interns can thrive, explore career paths, and build a strong foundation for their future.

Our internships are designed to be hands-on, providing interns with meaningful projects and responsibilities that directly impact BPN's mission and services. Whether you're a student seeking academic credit or a budding professional looking to gain practical experience, BPN offers a rewarding journey.

Possible Intern Positions at BPN:

While specific opportunities may vary based on organizational needs and ongoing projects, BPN may offer internships in areas such as:

  • Program Development Intern: Assist in researching, designing, and implementing new programs and initiatives that align with BPN's mission, such as new training modules, mentorship programs, or community outreach events.
  • Marketing & Communications Intern: Support BPN's outreach efforts by assisting with social media management, website content creation, newsletter development, press releases, and promotional materials.
  • Assistive Technology (AT) Support Intern: Work alongside our AT specialists, assisting with research on new technologies, developing training materials, and providing technical support to members. This role is ideal for those with a strong interest in AT and a desire to help others.
  • Event Planning & Coordination Intern: Help organize and execute BPN's virtual and in-person events, including professional development workshops, networking mixers, and conferences.
  • Research & Advocacy Intern: Contribute to research projects related to employment trends, accessibility best practices, and policy initiatives affecting the blind and visually impaired community. Support advocacy efforts through data collection and report writing.
  • Member Services Intern: Assist in managing member inquiries, maintaining member databases, and ensuring members have access to the resources and support they need.
  • Operations/Administrative Intern: Support the daily administrative functions of the organization, including data entry, scheduling, document management, and general office support.
  • Content Creation Intern: Develop engaging written or audio content for the BPN website, blog, or podcast, focusing on topics relevant to career development, assistive technology, and the experiences of blind and visually impaired professionals.

Benefits of Interning with BPN:

Interning with Blind Professionals Network offers a multitude of benefits that extend far beyond a typical internship:

  • Gain Practical, Hands-On Experience: Apply classroom knowledge to real-world projects, developing tangible skills directly applicable to your chosen career path.
  • Skill Development: Hone essential professional skills such as communication, teamwork, problem-solving, project management, and time management in a supportive environment.
  • Build a Professional Network: Connect with experienced professionals, mentors, and peers within the blind and visually impaired community and beyond, opening doors to future opportunities and collaborations.
  • Enhance Your Resume: Add valuable work experience to your professional profile, making you a more competitive candidate in the job market.
  • Explore Career Paths: Gain insights into various roles and industries, helping you clarify your career goals and identify areas of interest.
  • Mentorship Opportunities: Benefit from guidance and support from seasoned professionals who are passionate about empowering the next generation.
  • Increase Self-Confidence: Successfully navigating professional tasks and contributing to a meaningful mission will boost your self-assurance and readiness for future challenges.
  • Understand Workplace Dynamics: Learn professional etiquette, communication styles, and collaboration strategies within a real organizational setting.
  • Advocacy and Accessibility Focus: Gain a deeper understanding of workplace accommodations and accessibility best practices, and contribute to an organization that champions these principles.
  • Make a Meaningful Impact: Contribute directly to BPN's mission of empowering blind and visually impaired professionals, knowing your work helps improve lives and foster greater inclusion.
  • Potential for Future Opportunities: Successful internships can often lead to future employment opportunities or strong professional references.

Join Blind Professionals Network and embark on an internship that will not only advance your career but also connect you with a vibrant community dedicated to professional excellence.
