BVI Empowered

Welcome to BVI Empowered Podcast, sponsored by Blind Professionals Network!

Your host Sayyida Victoria and Joe Fleeks have an open discussion with diverse individuals in the blind and visually impaired (BVI)community to highlight their various levels of accomplishments, how they overcame challenges and reached their goals, despite their visual disability. Guests will share their journeys in hopes to empower and inspire all!

Subscribe to the BVI Empowered Podcast!

Success has many levels and can be measured with a variety of value. Whether personal or professional, we want to help spread awareness, share stories of struggle and triumph, and most importantly, show society that BVI individuals are people too, we just see differently!
