BPN Member of the Month

What is Member of the Month

BPN would like to recognize blind and visually impaired individuals that embodies the characteristics of a true “Blind Professional! These attributes include, but not limited to, positive attitude, passionate, motivated, determined, inspirational, ambitious, empowered, and so much more. BPN strongly believes sharing the stories of these individuals will help shine a positive light in the BVI community!

Each BPN member of the month chosen symbolizes the attributes above and has made an impact that inspires all!

MEMBER of the MONTH ` May 2022

Tricia Waechter

Houston, TX

Tricia Waechter is the founder and CEO of BLIND GIRL DESIGNS. She has retinitis pigmentosa, and has a 10° visual field remaining. Tricia’s entire career has been in the creative arts in either theater or fashion design. When the Retinitis Pigmentosa ended, her options of being able to actively work in a corporation in the creative field, she was devastated. Truthfully though, she had been aware of this possibility for decades, and enthusiastically traveled to all seven continents and 48 countries, which actually fit perfectly within her work. She is a big fan of bucket lists and crossing off stuff on bucket lists. After a rough patch of rapid sight loss, she worked through the resulting depression with a therapist and , Tricia rallied. She founded BLIND GIRL DESIGNS on January 1, 2021. Her primary goal is to normalize blindness within the community. Acceptance, love ,empathy, Optimism are her key beliefs and building blocks in this endeavor.
