BPN Employment Services: Empowering Blind and Visually Impaired Professionals to thrive!

Blind Professionals Network (BPN) is dedicated to empowering blind and visually impaired individuals to achieve their career aspirations. Through the BPN Employment Services program, members gain access to personalized, 1-on-1 coaching designed to navigate the unique challenges and opportunities in the job market for those with vision loss. Our experienced coaches provide tailored support to help you excel at every stage of your employment journey.

Our comprehensive 1-on-1 coaching includes:

  • Resume Writing: Crafting compelling resumes that highlight your skills, experience, and accomplishments in an accessible format. We work with you to ensure your resume stands out to employers and effectively communicates your value.
  • Interview Preparation: Developing strategies to confidently articulate your abilities and address potential questions related to your visual impairment. We'll practice interview techniques, discuss appropriate accommodations, and build your self-assurance for successful interactions.
  • Job Search Techniques: Learning effective and accessible methods for identifying suitable job opportunities, navigating online job boards, and leveraging professional networks. We help you refine your search strategy to target roles that align with your skills and career goals.
  • Career Exploration: Guiding you through the process of identifying your strengths, interests, and potential career paths. Whether you're just starting out or looking to make a career change, we help you explore options and set realistic, achievable goals.

Beyond these core services, the BPN Employment Services program also offers support in:

  • Disclosure Strategies: Providing guidance on when, how, and if to disclose your visual impairment to potential employers, helping you make informed decisions that align with your comfort level and the specific job opportunity. This includes discussing the advantages and disadvantages of different disclosure timings and approaches.
  • Assistive Technology (AT) Integration: Offering expert advice and training on identifying, utilizing, and advocating for the most effective assistive technologies to enhance your workplace productivity and accessibility.
  • Workplace Accommodation Advocacy: Empowering you to understand your rights and effectively advocate for reasonable accommodations in the workplace, ensuring an inclusive and supportive environment where you can perform your best.
  • Completing Applications: Receiving assistance with accurately and efficiently filling out online and physical job applications, ensuring all necessary information is provided clearly.
  • Networking Strategies: Developing effective approaches for building professional relationships, attending networking events, and utilizing platforms like LinkedIn to expand your professional circle.
  • Job Retention Strategies: Learning techniques and resources to not only secure a job but also thrive and advance in your chosen career, addressing any ongoing challenges that may arise.
  • Entrepreneurial Guidance: For those interested in self-employment, we can provide insights and resources to help develop a business plan and navigate the path to entrepreneurial success.
