BPN Virtual Support Groups

Blind Professionals Network (BPN) understands the unique challenges and opportunities faced by blind and visually impaired individuals in their professional and personal lives. To foster a strong, supportive community, BPN offers two distinct virtual support groups, accessible to all BPN members via Zoom conference call. Detailed access information for each group is sent directly to members via email. These groups provide a safe and relatable space for connection, shared learning, and mutual empowerment.

Here's a closer look at our support groups:

BPN Connect: A Relatable Platform for Blind and Visually Impaired Professionals

Join the Blind Professionals Network (BPN) every first Wednesday of the month for BPN Connect, a vital Zoom conference call designed for blind and visually impaired professionals. This is your dedicated space to unite, share experiences, and empower each other.

On BPN Connect, we delve into the unique challenges faced in the workplace and during employment searches. We openly discuss effective tips and strategies to overcome barriers, ensuring you're equipped with the tools to succeed. Our conversations also cover current events impacting the blind and visually impaired professional community, providing a platform for relevant discussions. We explore assistive technology that can enhance your workplace experience, sharing insights on the latest innovations and practical applications. Plus, stay informed about upcoming events, valuable trainings, and crucial employment opportunities, including job fairs and current job openings. This and much more awaits you in our supportive and relatable platform. Come connect with peers who understand your journey!

BPN Wellness Support Group:

The virtual BPN Wellness Support Group is designed specifically for our community. This vital initiative will focus on maintaining positive mental health, sharing effective coping strategies, and providing tips to help avoid depression and anxiety. It will also equip you with the tools to recognize symptoms associated with the unique challenges blind and visually impaired individuals face related to employment or unemployment.

Why This Group Matters

This support group offers a safe and understanding space to discuss common mental health challenges within the BVI community, including:

  • Navigating employment stressors: The emotional impact of job searching, workplace accommodations, and career progression.
  • Coping with societal perceptions: Addressing the mental toll of misconceptions or biases.
  • Building resilience: Developing strategies to overcome setbacks and maintain a positive outlook.
  • Managing daily living challenges: Discussing the mental health aspects of accessibility and independence.
  • Fostering connection: Reducing feelings of isolation by connecting with others who share similar experiences.

Facilitated by a Professional

This group is different from our BPN Connect monthly Peer Support Group as it will be facilitated by a Licensed Professional Counselor and/or Licensed Professional Counselor Associate , ensuring professional guidance and expert insights.

We believe this Wellness support group is an invaluable resource for our members, providing a dedicated space to prioritize mental well-being. We encourage you to join us and benefit from this unique opportunity.
